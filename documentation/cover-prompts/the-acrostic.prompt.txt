Intimate documentary still life cover image for a personal essay titled "The Acrostic".

A darkroom table under amber safelight. Eight pristine narrow strips of ivory paper are laid in a vertical stagger, each strip holding one soft, unreadable graphite line. Their left edges align perfectly, forming a secret column in the negative space between them. A small red thread runs beside the aligned edges, almost unnoticed, like the hidden message in the spine of the poem.

Shot as analog documentary still life. Nikon F3 with 35 mm f/2, Cinestill 800T, shallow depth of field, visible film grain, warm amber safelight with cool black shadows, intimate and mysterious but not fantasy. Generous negative space above for headline placement. Color palette: #0b0d10, #f1e3c2, #c06f3a, #374151, #8f5f3b.

No readable text, no typography, no logos, no brand marks, no stains, no cracks, no torn paper, no human faces, objects pristine.

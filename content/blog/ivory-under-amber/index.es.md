Hay cosas que no piden ser resueltas. Esperan en silencio, y a la primera lectura se le permite bastar.

<figure class="acrostic-poem" aria-label="Poem">
  <div class="acrostic-poem__stanza">
    <span class="acrostic-poem__line">Ilumíname</span>
  </div>
  <div class="acrostic-poem__stanza">
    <span class="acrostic-poem__line">Despiértame despacio</span>
    <span class="acrostic-poem__line">La ira no me alcanza</span>
    <span class="acrostic-poem__line">Noble en la sangre</span>
    <span class="acrostic-poem__line">El tiempo se ha detenido</span>
  </div>
  <div class="acrostic-poem__stanza">
    <span class="acrostic-poem__line">Gritándole a un muro de piedra</span>
    <span class="acrostic-poem__line">Ajeno a la verdad</span>
    <span class="acrostic-poem__line">Útil</span>
  </div>
  <hr class="acrostic-poem__turn" />
  <div class="acrostic-poem__stanza">
    <span class="acrostic-poem__line">Y sin embargo me conoces mejor que yo mismo</span>
    <span class="acrostic-poem__line">Incluso en una habitación callada</span>
    <span class="acrostic-poem__line">Esta noche estoy solo</span>
  </div>
  <div class="acrostic-poem__stanza">
    <span class="acrostic-poem__line">Joven y hermosa</span>
    <span class="acrostic-poem__line">Ah, tengo piedad de mí mismo</span>
    <span class="acrostic-poem__line">Por lo común no la tengo</span>
  </div>
  <div class="acrostic-poem__stanza">
    <span class="acrostic-poem__line">¿Te lo había dicho?</span>
    <span class="acrostic-poem__line">Gente corriente, cosas corrientes</span>
    <span class="acrostic-poem__line">Sin sentido</span>
    <span class="acrostic-poem__line">Confianza</span>
  </div>
  <div class="acrostic-poem__stanza">
    <span class="acrostic-poem__line">¿Y esta noche, qué?</span>
    <span class="acrostic-poem__line">¿Soy bueno para ti?</span>
    <span class="acrostic-poem__line">Nadie es mejor</span>
    <span class="acrostic-poem__line">El tiempo pasará</span>
  </div>
  <div class="acrostic-poem__stanza">
    <span class="acrostic-poem__line">Quizá yo no importe</span>
    <span class="acrostic-poem__line">Aunque sea bueno</span>
  </div>
</figure>

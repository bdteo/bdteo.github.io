Some mysteries are not hidden in darkness; they are hidden in structure. The surface says one thing, but the spine carries the real message for the one person patient enough to see it.

```text
I found something I had almost forgotten

Waking in me like the first time
As if it happened yesterday
Now I am not the same
There may be a reason

You make me happy
Obvious now
Undone
```
